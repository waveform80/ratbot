I'm a PhD student in the UK studing palaeobiochemistry. After getting into comics relatively recently I decided to try drawing some of my own. The Major Mass Spec series was the first one I did and is based on the experiences of, and people in my research group. The Ratbot Comics logo was inspired by my pet rats, Jay and Silent Bob.
